SEQUENTIAL ELEMENTS (FLIP-FLOPS)

D FLIP-FLOP

Captures input D on clock rising edge.

DCLK ↑Q
0↑0
1↑1
X—Q (hold)

T FLIP-FLOP (TOGGLE)

Toggles Q when T=1, holds when T=0.

TCLK ↑Q_next
0↑Q (hold)
1↑Q̄ (toggle)

SR FLIP-FLOP (SET-RESET)

S=Set, R=Reset. S=1 sets Q=1, R=1 sets Q=0.

SRCLK ↑Q_next
00↑Q (hold)
01↑0 (reset)
10↑1 (set)
11↑1 (S priority)

JK FLIP-FLOP

Versatile FF: J=Set, K=Reset, J=K=1 toggles.

JKCLK ↑Q_next
00↑Q (hold)
01↑0 (reset)
10↑1 (set)
11↑Q̄ (toggle)
